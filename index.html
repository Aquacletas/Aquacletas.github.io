More

Here's all the stuff I do.

I love creating stylised drawings that preserve moments, places and memories in a different, unique way! I also enjoy illustrating wildlife and nature, showcasing and promoting awareness on endangered species.

Bespoke portraits

I try to capture unique memories with my watercolour-inspired portraits! My most personal touch consists in creating empty, negative spaces, to allow all viewers to picture themselves in the moment, while showcasing the individualities of the portrayed.

Nature prints

I love to illustrate wildlife using a more abstract, personal style: all my animal and nature paintings are delineated using white strokes.

Landscapes and cityscapes

I also paint landscapes and cityscapes as another way to showcase my memories and bucket-listed places.

Tote bags, postcards and more!

I often create seasonal and more utilitary items based on my illustrations!

About me

Lovely to see you here!
My name is Ana and I'm a PhD student based in London. I've always loved painting, and during the lockdown I finally had some free time to go back to my passion!

I started painting my favourite places and memories from past trips, as well as some wildlife, and over time I discovered my own painting style; a mix between abstract lines with watercolour washes.

After some time, I decided to sell my favourite designs and offer custom portraits and art through Etsy... I would have never imagined that what started as a hobbie would end up in creating beautiful gifts for all kinds of people!
